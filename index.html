Privacy Policy

NoBuy Day Day — No Spend Day Tracker · Effective Date: March 17, 2026

Overview

NoBuy Day is designed with privacy at its core. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your information stays on your device.

Data Storage

All spending records, streak data, and category preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Data Collection

NoBuy Day does not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Financial or payment data
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers
  • Advertising data

Notifications

NoBuy Day may send local notifications to remind you to log your daily spending status. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not require any server communication. You can enable or disable notifications at any time in the app settings or iOS Settings.

Third-Party Services

NoBuy Day does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

UserDefaults

NoBuy Day uses UserDefaults to store app preferences such as onboarding completion status and notification settings. This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted anywhere.

Children's Privacy

NoBuy Day does not collect any data from any user, including children. The app is safe for use by people of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
